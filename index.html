Story

I’m Sofiane, also known as Shoji (翔司). I grew up between noise, silence, ambition and doubt. I learned to move, to code, to perform, to switch languages – and to rebuild myself after difficult moments.

Who I am

Student in data / tech, former semi-pro volley player, dancer, theatre kid, language addict. I’m building a life between France, Japan and beyond – with a focus on environment & information studies.

Languages

French (native), English, Japanese (conversational, aiming N2), LSF, JSL (Japanese Sign Language, in progress), plus early steps in Korean and Mandarin.

What I care about

Connection. Accessibility. Movement. Expression. I love spaces where art, tech and people overlap – like Keio SFC: not just classes, but projects, circles, and real impact.
